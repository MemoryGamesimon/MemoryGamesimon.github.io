BRAIN TRAIN

Instructions:
1. Wait for the specific color(s) to stop blinking.
Then click/tap the respective color(s) as follows.
The number of blink varies on the number in the middle of the colors.
2. If you lost track of the color blink patterns,
click/tap the restart button.
3. One wrong tap and the game is over.
Follow the consequence that will appear on your screen after the score.
